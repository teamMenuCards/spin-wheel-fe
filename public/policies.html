Privacy Policy – Menu-cards.com

Effective Date: 16 June 2025

Business Name: Welnia Foods Pvt Ltd (Trading as Menu-cards.com)

1. About Us

MenuCards (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides QR-code menus, direct ordering, payments, and feedback tools that restaurants use to serve diners both on-premise and online. We operate menu-cards.com, the MenuCards dashboard, APIs, and related WhatsApp Business flows.

2. Information We Collect

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide order, payment, and feedback services (contractual).
  • Send WhatsApp notifications (order status, feedback prompts) only to users who opted-in.
  • Analyse aggregate usage to improve features (legitimate interest).
  • Comply with accounting & tax laws (legal obligation).

4. WhatsApp Business Compliance

We use WhatsApp strictly as per Meta's Business Messaging Policy. We:

  • Collect opt-in via check-box or QR order flow.
  • Use pre-approved templates limited to order updates, feedback, or requested info.
  • Do not spam, resell data, or cold message users.
  • Allow users to opt out anytime by replying “STOP”.
